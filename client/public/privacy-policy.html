Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Surri ("we", "our", or "the app") is a free multiplayer card game developed by Xure Solutions. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

What We Collect

We use Firebase Analytics (by Google) to collect anonymous usage data, including:

  • App interactions (screens viewed, games played, features used)
  • Device information (device model, OS version, screen size)
  • Anonymous device identifiers

What We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • No location data
  • No photos, files, or contacts
  • No financial or payment information

Display Names

When you play multiplayer, you enter a display name. This name is stored only in server memory during your game session and is never saved to any database or persisted after the session ends.

How We Use Data

Anonymous analytics data helps us:

  • Understand which features are popular
  • Identify and fix bugs
  • Improve the game experience

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties. Firebase Analytics data is processed by Google under their privacy policy.

Data Retention

Firebase Analytics data is retained for 14 months (the Firebase default), after which it is automatically deleted.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your analytics data by contacting us at the email below. You can also reset your advertising ID on your device to disassociate future data from past usage.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we collect only anonymous analytics, no personally identifiable information is gathered from any user.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
