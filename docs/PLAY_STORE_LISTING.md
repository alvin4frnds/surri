## Full Description

Surri is a beloved Indian card game — a dealer-centric Spades variant where strategy, teamwork, and bold bidding decide the winner.

🃏 HOW TO PLAY
• 4 players in 2 teams compete in a trick-taking card game
• Each round, players bid on how many tricks they can win
• The bidder chooses the trump suit — any suit, not just spades!
• Win tricks, break your opponents' bids, and avoid accumulating score
• First team to force 3 unique players to lose is out — the last one standing wins!

⚡ FEATURES
• Play online with friends using a simple room code
• Fill empty seats with smart AI bots (0–3 bots)
• Real-time multiplayer powered by WebSocket
• Support signals: ask your partner for Major, Minor, or Pass before bidding
• TRAM: claim all remaining tricks mid-play if you're confident!
• Works on phones, tablets, and desktop browsers

🎯 GAME RULES
• 2 teams of 2: seats across from each other are partners
• Bidding starts at 10 (or 8 if forced) — bid higher to control the round
• Bid 10+: your partner's hand is revealed to everyone; you control their plays
• Bid 13: instant win or lose — no score needed!
• Only the dealer's team tracks score — high score is bad (52+ = loss)
• Dealer rotates based on round outcomes and score thresholds

📱 PLAY ANYWHERE
Surri works seamlessly as an Android app. Create a room, share the code with friends, and start playing in seconds!
